# Hotel Booking Demand Analysis

## Overview

The Hotel Booking Demand Analysis project explores hotel booking data to understand customer booking behavior, cancellation patterns, seasonal demand, pricing trends, and hotel performance. The objective is to generate actionable insights that help improve occupancy, optimize pricing strategies, and reduce booking cancellations.

---

## Business Problem

Hotels face challenges such as high booking cancellation rates, seasonal fluctuations in demand, and inefficient room allocation. This project analyzes historical booking data to identify trends and patterns that support better business decisions and revenue optimization.

---

## Objectives

- Analyze hotel booking trends.
- Identify booking cancellation patterns.
- Study customer booking behavior.
- Understand seasonal demand across different months.
- Analyze lead time and its impact on cancellations.
- Evaluate Average Daily Rate (ADR) distribution.
- Compare reserved and assigned room types.
- Provide business recommendations based on data-driven insights.

---

## Dataset

- **Source:** Kaggle - Hotel Booking Demand Dataset
- **Total Records:** 119,390
- **Total Features:** 32

The dataset contains information about hotel bookings, customer demographics, booking channels, room types, lead time, cancellations, ADR, and reservation status.
